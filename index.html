I'm Allison, an education reporter and data journalist.

In addition to being a journo and data nerd, I'm an amateur baker just trying to make a successful sourdough bread.

Recent work

Restraint and seclusion in schools

After hearing anecdotes about students in schools being held down or confined to a room, I started pulling public records to understand the scope of the use of the practices. The first major finding: the state's largest school district had significantly underreported the number of times restraint and seclusion were being used - and it knew it had done so.

Student discipline

I have been focused on reporting on disparities in student discipline in schools. Mining district data sets has yielded stories never been told before, including about how schools don't always follow the district's code of conduct when doling out punishments - sometimes handing out suspensions for as simple a thing as being tardy.

Back-to-school changes map

I worked with another education reporter and our digital developer to put together this interactive map of changes for the start of a new school year. The interactive earned a 1st-place award from the Kentucky Press Association.
